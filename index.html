About us

Baixue Solutions is a new company specialized in bio-ethanol production.
Our priority is to offer a quality product for an affordable price.
In order to assure the "Baixue quality", our sugarcanes
undergo a secret process in our labs before being processed in ethanol.

Precautionary measure :

We have received multiple reports concerning
ethanol barrels showing signs of self-awareness.

If you think one of our barrel is sentient,
please report it to our customer service.
A member of our staff will recover the
barrel and replace it with a new one.

  • Don't interact with the ethanol
  • Don't listen to what it says
